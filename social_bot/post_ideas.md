# Project Team Wear — Creative Post Playbook

## Content Pillars (rotate through these)

| Pillar | Frequency | Goal |
|--------|-----------|------|
| 🏆 Kit Showcase | 2x/week | Show off designs, drive quote requests |
| 🤝 Team Spotlight | 1x/week | Social proof, community feel |
| 💡 Education | 1x/week | Build trust, position as experts |
| 🔥 Culture/Motivation | 1x/week | Brand identity, engagement |
| 🎨 Behind The Scenes | 1x/week | Humanise the brand |

---

## Post Formats Beyond the Jersey

### 1. JERSEY + PLAYER (Editorial)
**Style:** `editorial`
**Prompt idea:** "Split composition — left half shows jersey flat lay, right half shows athlete wearing it mid-game, dark dramatic lighting, orange colour grade"
**Caption hook:** "Same design. Two different games."

### 2. COLOUR REVEAL
**Style:** `product`
**Prompt idea:** "Multiple colour variants of the same jersey design arranged in a fan/spread on dark surface, each colourway labelled with a small tag, studio lighting"
**Caption hook:** "Your colours. Your call."

### 3. TEXTURE CLOSE-UP
**Style:** `product`
**Prompt idea:** "Extreme close-up macro photography of custom jersey fabric — sublimation print detail, stitching quality, breathable mesh texture, dark moody lighting"
**Caption hook:** "Built to last. Built to perform."

### 4. TEAM ARRIVAL (Lifestyle)
**Style:** `lifestyle`
**Prompt idea:** "Sports team walking into a venue or field in matching custom kits, shot from behind, golden hour light, community and belonging energy"
**Caption hook:** "When the kit drops and everyone shows up matching."

### 5. QUOTE CARD (Editorial)
**Style:** `editorial`
**Prompt idea:** "Bold typographic poster on dark background — large white text reads 'YOUR TEAM. YOUR DESIGN. NO LIMITS.' with orange PTW logo bottom right, geometric accents"
**Caption hook:** *(caption IS the design — minimal text post)*

### 6. BEFORE / AFTER CONCEPT
**Style:** `editorial`
**Prompt idea:** "Split screen — left: generic blank plain training kit, right: stunning custom PTW design in full colour. Clean graphic divider line. 'Generic vs Custom' implied not stated."
**Caption hook:** "Stop blending in."

### 7. FOUNDER/PROCESS (Behind the Scenes)
**Style:** `behind-scenes`
**Prompt idea:** "Hands reviewing jersey design on a tablet screen, colour swatches nearby, warm studio desk lighting, authentic workspace feel"
**Caption hook:** "Every kit starts with a conversation."

### 8. STAT CARD
**Style:** `editorial`
**Prompt idea:** "Bold graphic card — dark background, large orange number '14' with text 'days to your custom kit' below, clean geometric layout, Project Team Wear branding"
**Caption hook:** "14 days. That's it."

### 9. SPORT-SPECIFIC SHOWCASE
**Style:** `action`
**Prompt idea:** "Handball player in mid-throw wearing custom orange Project Team Wear kit, indoor court, dramatic side lighting, motion blur on ball"
**Caption hook:** "Built for the court."

### 10. SATISFIED TEAM (Social Proof)
**Style:** `lifestyle`
**Prompt idea:** "Sports team celebrating or posing together in matching custom kits, wide shot, genuine joy, outdoor Australian setting, late afternoon light"
**Caption hook:** "Another club. Another kit. Another team that looks the part."

## Caption Formulas That Work

**For product posts:**
[Bold statement about the design] + [what makes it custom] + CTA to DM

**For lifestyle posts:**
[Relatable team moment] + [what Project Team Wear stands for] + tag your club

**For educational posts:**
[Common mistake clubs make] + [how PTW solves it] + DM for a free quote

**For motivation posts:**
[Short punchy quote] + [1-2 lines tying it back to sport/teamwear] + hashtags only
